Cthuloot is a coop multiplayer arcade game.

A band of greedy adventurers are given a mission by a curious entity: they must join forces to find mythical treasures in lost and well-guarded places.

The path to these relics will be filled with traps, puzzles and creatures from another dimension.

Finding those treasure and getting them out will require some working brain cells, cooperation, a good pair of shoes and... a lovely voice (for the chanting)!

  • TEAM UP: Play with friends and family members!
  • COOP CAMPAIGN: 4 chapters, 75+ levels full of surprises
  • MULTIPLAYER: Online or local, up to 4, no split screen
  • FAMILY FRIENDLY: no violence, no combat, light horror theme
  • PULP CTHULHU: a modern, light and fun take on the Lovecraft's lore

Coming in 2024!
