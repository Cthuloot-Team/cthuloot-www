CTHULOOT

**Cthuloot** is a coop multiplayer (local and online) arcade game, where a band of greedy adventurers are given a mission by a curious entity:
they must join forces to find mythical treasures in lost and well-guarded places.

The path to these relics will be filled with traps, puzzles and creatures from another dimension.

Finding those treasure and getting them out will require some working brain cells, cooperation, a good pair of shoes and... a lovely voice (for the chanting)!

  • MULTIPLAYER: all game modes playable with friends and family, up to 4 players (online or local)
  • CAMPAIGN: 4 chapters, 75+ levels full of surprises
